How it works

Three steps, one sitting

  1. 01

    Scan

    Open a live video call and point your camera at the broken item. Our on-device model flags likely faults in real time as you move around it — no upload, no waiting for results.

  2. 02

    Match

    If the AI needs a second opinion, the call escalates instantly to an available technician nearby, who joins already seeing what was flagged — not starting from scratch.

  3. 03

    Fix

    Get a live parts price and stock comparison from local shops, book the repair, and track it step by step until it's done — and see exactly what that repair saved.
